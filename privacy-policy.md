# Privacy Policy — Game Score

*Last updated: June 2026*

## Data Collection

Game Score does not collect any personal data. All data (games, players, scores) is stored **locally on your device only** and is never transmitted to any external server.

## Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

## Permissions

The app may request access to device storage solely to allow importing and exporting game history JSON files, at the user's explicit request.
